The Best Movies According to Me (Aryan)

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Fitoor

Movie about a Poor but talented boy falls in love with a girl from an affluent family.

Kahaani 2

A very emotional film depicting a mother's unconditional love for her daughter.

Baar Baar Dekho

The highs and lows of a romantic relationship are examined through flashbacks and flash-forwards.
